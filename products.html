PropaStakes

This product allows our customers to own a unit stake of our real estate projects and start their journey to wealth creation. With Propa-Stakes, our customers can have access to highly profitable opportunities that offer both capital gains and cash flow.
How It Works
Propa-Stakes is a system that breaks down properties into the smallest units of cost and investment that allows our customers to own a part of massive real estate projects with the similar structure of owning stocks.
However, Propa-Stakes allows you to own a part of a real and tangible investment with huge market and an ever-rising valuation. Propa-Stakes is an asset banking mode that allows you save your money outside the bank while you beat inflation due to property value appreciation.
